How credits work

  • Each new listing found costs 1 credit. The first check of every new search is free — you only pay for listings that appear after that.
  • Starting searches and re-checks that find nothing new are free.
  • Credits refill automatically every week.
  • Cancelling a recurring search frees up one of your search slots.
  • Upgrades take effect immediately — downgrades at the end of the billing period.
